# **App Name**: Five Years of Us

## Core Features:

- Opening Screen: Display a welcome screen showing the time span and a brief intro.
- Home Screen: Present navigation cards leading to different apology sections.
- Timeline Display: Show a timeline of the relationship from 2020 to 2025 with key memories.
- Apology Sections: Display apology sections, including reflections on where things went wrong and expressions of feelings.
- Database storage: Use Firestore to store and retrieve the app content, including timeline details and apology sections. Store text under timeline, apology_sections and final_promise
- Final Promise Screen: Conclude with a soft promise and gratitude message with no call to action.
- Gentle Message Crafter: Allow the user to refine the message they wish to communicate on the various apology screens by editing the provided text via an AI-powered tool. The AI ensures the message remains within the defined emotional goals.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm blush pink (#F2B7B7) to evoke romance and softness.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC), providing a calm and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted lavender (#D8BFD8), used sparingly for highlights and gentle contrast.
- Heading font: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) for a handwritten cursive style, giving a personal touch.
- Body text font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for an elegant and readable feel.
- Minimalistic icons, mainly for timeline markers, kept soft and unobtrusive.
- Spacious layout with generous use of whitespace to promote calmness and reflection.
- Slow fade-ins for content appearance and subtle floating light particles in the background to add a gentle, dreamlike quality.
